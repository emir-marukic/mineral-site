ABOUT US

The company Mineral d.o.o. has been present on the Slovenian and international markets since 1946. In two locations, in Pohorje and on the outskirts of the Ljubljana basin, it extracts native natural stone. Stone processing is carried out at three locations. In 2018, Mineral expanded its activity to the field of high-rise buildings on the market in Germany. The company Mineral d.o.o. constantly invests in new technologies and in the development of staff expertise.
